How It Works

1. Data Collection

IoT sensors continuously monitor temperature, humidity, noise levels, air quality, and lighting conditions throughout your space.

2. Machine Learning

Our ensemble of ML models (including XGBoost and Random Forest) processes the sensor data to identify comfort patterns.

3. Comfort Prediction

The system generates a real-time comfort score (2-5) and identifies factors affecting occupant comfort.

4. Automated Optimization

HVAC, lighting, and other systems are automatically adjusted to maximize comfort while minimizing energy consumption.

About SmartComfort

Our Mission

SmartComfort aims to revolutionize indoor environmental quality by leveraging machine learning to predict and optimize comfort levels in real-time. Our system bridges the gap between human comfort and energy efficiency in smart buildings, particularly in climate-sensitive regions like Manali.
